Icoziv is a curated collection of over 300 developer and design icons that helps you instantly showcase your technical skills. Whether you're building your portfolio, creating social media content, or just want to flex your tech stack, Icoziv provides high-quality, consistent icons for all major technologies.

Our mission is to make it easier for developers and designers to visually represent their expertise. With a simple search and copy interface, you can quickly find and use icons for frameworks, languages, tools, and platforms that power modern development.

Built with performance and accessibility in mind, Icoziv offers a smooth, responsive experience across all devices. The collection is continuously updated to include the latest technologies and trends in the developer ecosystem.

Curated Collection

Over 300 carefully selected icons covering all major programming languages, frameworks, tools, and platforms used in modern development.

Easy to Use

Simple search functionality with instant copy-to-clipboard feature. No complex setup required - just search, select, and copy.

High Quality

All icons are optimized for web use with consistent sizing, styling, and format. Perfect for portfolios, READMEs, and presentations.

Regular Updates

New icons are added regularly to keep up with the evolving tech landscape. Community contributions are welcome!

Performance Focused
